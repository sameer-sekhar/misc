SOME HOT TIPS TO THE EXAMINEE

1.   Take the test seriously. It can help you.

2.   Don't panic in between the exam. You have enough time to attempt the questions.

3.   For your convenience a set of instructions are given. If you want to see the
      instructions in between the exam, just DOUBLE CLICK anywhere on the PAGE.

4.   Try your best to attempt as many questions as possible.

5.   Your success mostly depends on your effort and the rest is luck..

6.   Keep cool and give it a go....!
